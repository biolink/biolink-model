    • Range: Publication

    Comments

    • The notion of a ‘Publication’ is considered broadly to include any document made available for public consumption. It covers journal issues, individual articles, and books - and also things like article pre-prints, white papers, patents, drug labels, web pages, protocol documents, etc.
